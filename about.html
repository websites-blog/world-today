THE WAR IN UKRAINE

On February 24, 2022 Russia has launch a full-scale invasion in Ukraine their objective is to occupy the entire country. The invasion has began early at dawn with an infantry division, armored vehicle and air support as dozens of missile attacks across the border of Ukraine then suddenly turn into a battlefield. Where the Russian army has continue to evoke any military operations in different areas from north, south and east and the Ukrainian forces fight especially to defend their country. And the war has killed a thousands of civilians which has also led in more than eight millions citizens of Ukraine left their homeland.

But until now the war between the two countries has still continue where the armed forces of Ukraine is now conducting their counteroffensive, while the Russian army holding their positions to repelled Ukraine's attack. It has now been nearly two years of fighting and cruelty but still there is no signs of ending. The armed conflict of Ukraine and Russia that has started a year and a half before and now it will nearly enter it second year and no one knows how long it will going to take. As the war continue many citizens in Ukraine also continue to suffer and which most of them have lost especially their famillies, relatives, love ones and even dearest friends. But still the war between the two states has not ending and continue to expand more.
